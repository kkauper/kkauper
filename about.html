Creating holistic design concepts in the digital world

I’m a communication designer with a keen focus on UX/UI and a passion for learning new technologies. As a self-taught developer, I’m always excited to explore the latest trends in the field and bring my own unique perspective to the table.

With several years of experience in digital design, I’ve had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of clients and projects, from small startups to large corporations. I take pride in creating thoughtful and innovative solutions that not only meet but exceed the needs of my clients.

My skillset includes a wide range of design tools and software, including Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and Figma, to name a few. I’m also proficient in front-end web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, PHP as well as a bit of jQuery and Vue.JS, allowing me to bring my designs to life and ensure a seamless user experience.
